history Some History

I am currently a Software R&D Engineer at Compliance Robotics, a startup that builds soft-robots.

Before that, I was a R&D Engineer at Flanders Make and worked on several projects for the industry 4.0. A few years back I worked as a Customer Experience R&D engineer at Worldline exploring the future user experiences around payments

During my previous postdoc at the University of Waterloo in Canada, I explored the use of gestures to enrich the interaction vocabulary with mobile devices. At ESTIA, I also investigated the use of AR to assist field operators in maintenance tasks. Gestural mid-air interaction was my main research interest during my PhD in which I proposed a design framework to explore sub-spaces for mid-air interaction.
